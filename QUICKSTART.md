### Step 1: Load the Extension
1. Open Chrome and go to: `chrome://extensions/`
2. Toggle **Developer mode** ON (top-right)
3. Click **Load unpacked**
4. Select folder: `C:\Users\canwa\WebstormProjects\EnglishLearning\dist`

### Step 2: Try It Out
1. Visit any English website (e.g., Wikipedia, news site)
2. Select any word with your mouse
3. Click the green **Explain** button
4. Click **Add to Learning Diary**

### Step 3: View Your Diary
1. Click the extension icon in Chrome toolbar
2. See your saved words
3. Filter by: Today / This Week / This Month / This Year / All
